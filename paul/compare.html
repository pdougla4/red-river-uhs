What to watch for

The top-left (Case 0) is the control. Compare the extent and depth of the CO₂ plume in each of the other three panels against that baseline at the same moment in reservoir time. If the enhancement hypothesis holds, treated cases should show the flood front reach further and sweep more of the reservoir by the same point in the twenty-five-year operation.

Each frame represents roughly one month of simulated reservoir time. Use the scrub bar to move to a specific moment and freeze the comparison for detailed inspection. Breakthrough events at the offset producers appear as pressure and saturation changes near the east and west edges of the model.
